
## Examples ##

Scan-matching:

	$ sm2 < in.log > out.log

where `in.log` may be in either Carmen or JSON format.

Creating a pdf:

	$ carmen2pdf -use odometry  -in in.log -out out.pdf
	$ carmen2pdf -use estimate  -in in.log -out out.pdf
	$ carmen2pdf -use true_pose -in in.log -out out.pdf



## Examining one particular matching (video) ##

To zoom on one particular matching, write a journal:

	$ sm2 -file_jj journal.txt < in.log > out.log

Extract what you are interested in from the journal:

	$ json_extract -nth 13 < journal.txt > matching13.txt

Create animation:

	$ sm_icp_animation -in matching13.txt -out matching13.fig
	$ create_video.rb matching13.fig.desc
